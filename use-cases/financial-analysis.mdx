## Financial Analysis Workflows

### Quarterly Earnings Deep Dive

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Analysis">
    ```text
    You: "Analyze Apple's latest quarterly earnings performance"
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Assistant Response">
    ```text
    Assistant: I'll analyze Apple's latest quarterly earnings by examining their most recent 10-Q filing.
    
    🔧 Tools used: get_cik_by_ticker, get_submissions, get_company_concepts
    
    📊 Key Findings:
    • Q4 2024 Revenue: $119.58 billion (+6% YoY)
    • Net Income: $33.92 billion (+11% YoY)
    • iPhone Revenue: $69.7 billion (+6% YoY)
    • Services Revenue: $24.2 billion (+12% YoY)
    • Gross Margin: 46.2% (improved from 45.0% last year)
    
    📈 Analysis: Strong performance across all segments with particularly robust Services growth
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Follow-up Query">
    ```text
    You: "How does this compare to the same quarter last year?"
    
    Assistant: Comparing Q4 2024 vs Q4 2023:
    
    🔧 Tools used: get_xbrl_frames, get_company_concepts
    
    📊 Year-over-Year Growth:
    • Total Revenue: +6% ($119.58B vs $113.0B)
    • iPhone: +6% ($69.7B vs $65.8B)
    • Mac: +2% ($7.7B vs $7.6B)
    • iPad: -7% ($6.4B vs $6.9B)
    • Services: +12% ($24.2B vs $21.6B)
    • Wearables: +1% ($11.5B vs $11.4B)
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Financial Ratio Analysis

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Profitability Ratios">
    ```text
    You: "Calculate Apple's key profitability ratios"
    
    Assistant: I'll calculate Apple's profitability ratios using their latest financial data.
    
    🔧 Tools used: get_cik_by_ticker, get_company_concepts
    
    📊 Profitability Metrics (Q4 2024):
    • Gross Profit Margin: 46.2%
    • Operating Margin: 31.5%
    • Net Profit Margin: 28.4%
    • Return on Assets (ROA): 33.1%
    • Return on Equity (ROE): 171.9%
    ```

  </Tab>

    ```text

    • Current Ratio: 0.95
    • Quick Ratio: 0.91
    • Cash & Equivalents: $29.9 billion
    • Working Capital: -$5.2 billion
    ```

  </Tab>

### Time Series Analysis

```text
You: "Show me Apple's revenue trend over the past 5 years with quarterly data"
Assistant: Analyzing risk factors from Apple's latest 10-K filing.

🔧 Tools used: get_cik_by_ticker, get_submissions, get_filing_txt_sections

📊 Key Risk Categories Identified:
• Supply chain dependencies (China manufacturing)
• Regulatory compliance and privacy laws
• Intense competition in tech markets
• Foreign exchange rate fluctuations
• Intellectual property protection
• Cybersecurity and data privacy

⚠️ Top Concerns:
• 90% of manufacturing in China creates geopolitical risk
• New EU and US privacy regulations affecting data use
• Competition from Samsung, Google in mobile markets
```

## Best Practices

<Info>
  For accurate financial analysis, always verify data against official filings and consider using multiple data points for confirmation.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Validation">
    Cross-reference calculated ratios with published financial statements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Peer Selection">
    Choose appropriate industry peers with similar business models
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Time Period Consistency">
    Ensure all comparison data uses the same reporting periods
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contextual Analysis">
    Consider macroeconomic factors and industry-specific trends
  </Step>
</Steps>
