## Best Practices

<Info>
  For accurate financial analysis, always verify data against official filings and consider using multiple data points for confirmation.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Validation">
    Cross-reference calculated ratios with published financial statements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Peer Selection">
    Choose appropriate industry peers with similar business models
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Time Period Consistency">
    Ensure all comparison data uses the same reporting periods
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contextual Analysis">
    Consider macroeconomic factors and industry-specific trends
  </Step>
</Steps>
