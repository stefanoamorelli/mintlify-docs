## Best Practices

<Info>
  Investment research requires combining quantitative analysis with qualitative insights from SEC filings.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Multiple Data Points">
    Use various financial metrics and ratios for comprehensive analysis
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Time Series Analysis">
    Look at trends over multiple years, not just single periods
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Peer Comparison">
    Always compare companies within the same industry
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Qualitative Analysis">
    Read management commentary and risk factors carefully
  </Step>
</Steps>
