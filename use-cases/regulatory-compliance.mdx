## Compliance Monitoring Workflows

### Filing Deadline Tracking

```text
You: "Monitor Apple for any late filings or compliance issues in their recent SEC submissions"

Assistant: I'll check Apple's recent SEC filing compliance and any potential issues.

🔧 Tools used: get_cik_by_ticker, get_submissions, get_filing_txt_sections

📊 Compliance Status:
• All required filings submitted on time
• 10-K filed: September 28, 2024 (on schedule)
• Latest 10-Q: October 31, 2024 (within deadline)
• 8-K filings: 12 in past year (all timely)

✅ No compliance issues detected
• No amended filings (indicating errors)
• No NT (Non-Timely) filings
• All deadlines met for fiscal year 2024
```

### Insider Trading Monitoring

```text
You: "Track recent insider trading activity at Apple and flag any large transactions"

Assistant: I'll analyze recent Form 4 filings for insider trading activity at Apple.

🔧 Tools used: get_submissions, get_filing_txt_sections

📊 Insider Trading Summary (Last 30 days):
• Total Form 4 filings: 18
• Insiders trading: 7 executives/directors
• Total value: $142.3 million

🚩 Large Transactions Flagged:
• Tim Cook (CEO): Sold 511,000 shares @ $189.50 ($96.8M)
  - Part of pre-arranged 10b5-1 plan
  - Filed within 2 business days ✅

• Luca Maestri (CFO): Sold 85,000 shares @ $188.25 ($16.0M)
  - Scheduled vesting and sale
  - Proper disclosure ✅

🔍 All transactions appear compliant with SEC rules
```
